# 核心发现

该研究开发Sequence Display技术，实现无偏倚、大规模的蛋白质序列-功能景观映射，为机器学习引导的定向进化和蛋白质工程提供高质量训练数据集，突破传统随机突变筛选的深度与分辨率限制。

# 摘要

Designing proteins with desired functions or properties represents a core goal in synthetic biology and drug discovery. Sequence Display enables unbiased, large-scale mapping of protein sequence-activity landscapes, providing rich datasets for machine-learning-guided protein engineering. Directed evolution traditionally relies on random mutagenesis and screening, which limits the depth and resolution of sequence space exploration.
